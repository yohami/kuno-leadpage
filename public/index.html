How to Use
This Book

This book is a system.

I wrote it in classic hero journey — from rags to riches, from starting point to end. It is this way so it can find someone, you, at the common denominator place and guide you through the steps, covering every gap and minimizing friction.

But this is not the truth.

You have your own story — your own oscillation, your own lights and shadows and things you know and things you don't want to know yet. The knowledge, the system, is fractal. Small pieces that recombine and reveal their true nature at all times, regardless of how surface or deep you go.

  • You can use this book and go step by step. Do the exercises as written.
  • Or you can skim through it, find something that resonates, and focus there.

You're free.

Once you're done you'll realize that every single page had everything you ever needed. This is serious, but fun work. The payoff is beyond what you can imagine.

Play to win, and see you on the other side.

Before You Decide

Common Questions

Is this just another self-help book? +

No. Most self-help books tell you what you're missing. This one tells you what you are.

Is this also for women or only men? +

It's written from a man's perspective, about men's problems. But women who read it tend to recognize themselves in it. Sometimes uncomfortably so.

592 pages is a lot — do I have to read it all to get results? +

No. But you have to live one page completely.

I've tried everything. Why would this work? +

Because you haven't tried this. And also — "I've tried everything" is the problem. That's not a history of effort. That's a story you're using to pre-fail.

What if it doesn't work for me? +

Then you'll know something true about yourself instead of something comfortable. That's still a win.

I'm not into pickup or seduction — is this still relevant? +

The book isn't about pickup. Pickup is just where the ideas got tested. It's about why you shrink, why you chase, why you work uphill and call it effort. That happens at work, with friends, with yourself. The seduction stuff is just the clearest mirror.

Will this help me get laid? +

Yes. But that's the small prize.

Who is Yohami and why should I trust him? +

He's not a guru with a system to sell you. He's a guy who went deep into the problem, broke it apart, and wrote down what he found. Trust him or don't. The ideas either land or they don't. That's the only test that matters.

Is this for me? +

I don't know. Are you actually looking to fix things or just browsing?

KU/NO is for people who know something is off but can't name it yet. It's for you if you're ready to cut the BS and win.

KU/NO is not for people who want to feel understood without changing anything.
